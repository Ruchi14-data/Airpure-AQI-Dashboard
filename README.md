# Airpure-AQI-Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project was developed for AirPure Innovations to analyze air quality trends across India using Power BI.
The dashboard combines AQI, health, vehicle, and EV adoption datasets to identify pollution hotspots, understand seasonal trends, evaluate health impacts, and explore the relationship between EV adoption and air quality.
The project includes interactive dashboards and primary analysis to support data-driven decision making.

## Objectives

- Analyze AQI patterns across Indian states and cities.
- Identify the most and least polluted regions.
- Understand seasonal variations in air quality.
- Explore AQI-related health impacts.
- Assess vehicle growth and EV adoption trends.
- Answer business questions through primary analysis.

## Key Insights

- Delhi emerged as the highest-risk region with consistently high AQI levels.
- PM2.5 and PM10 are the most dominant pollutants across India.
- November to January recorded the worst air quality in most states.
- Higher AQI levels were associated with increased health burdens.
- Karnataka, Kerala, and Maharashtra showed relatively better AQI alongside higher EV adoption rates.
- EV adoption may contribute to improved air quality, but other factors such as industrial activity and urbanization also influence pollution levels.
